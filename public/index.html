Mathematics, Machine Learning, Data Science

I am a mathematician born data scientist/Machine Learning researcher currently working on building automated trading systems at The Voleon Group where I apply state-of-the-art machine learning techniques to develop predictive models for real-world problems in finance. Part of my job is developing and maintaining all components of the relevant pipeline ranging from data preparation through model development to production software implementation.
